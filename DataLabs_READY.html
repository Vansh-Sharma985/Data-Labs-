Hi, I am Vansh Sharma — a B.Com student from India with a real passion for data and technology. I am a Certified Data Analyst, certified by 4 Achievers, which gave me strong foundations in data analysis, Excel, SQL, Power BI, and business intelligence.

I built Data Labs entirely from scratch using my own ideas and creativity, and brought it to life with the help of Claude AI (by Anthropic). I had no prior professional software development experience — I started from absolute zero, learned everything on the go, and figured out every single feature step by step. From the UI design to AI-powered reports, geo maps, chart filters, data connectors, and share links — all of it is my original concept built by me.

My goal was simple: make professional data analytics accessible to everyone who does not know how to code. Whether you are a student building your first portfolio, a professional stuck in Excel spreadsheets, or someone preparing for a data analyst interview — Data Labs was made for you.
